Projects

⭐ Featured Project
Power BI Sales Dashboard

A fully interactive business intelligence dashboard built to analyze sales performance across regions, categories, and time periods. Developed a complete data workflow starting from raw, uncleaned data — through transformation, star schema modeling with fact and dimension tables — all the way to compelling visual reports.

The dashboard tracks 1K orders, $33.93M total revenue, 86K units sold, and $915K in freight. Includes dynamic maps showing sales by territory, KPI comparisons by product category (Bikes, Components, Clothing), order status breakdowns, and year-over-year trend analysis to support data-driven decision-making.
